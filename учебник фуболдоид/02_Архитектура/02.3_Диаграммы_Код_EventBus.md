## 🔄 Диаграмма последовательности: событие мяча

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Ball as BallMotion
    participant Bus as IGameEventBus
    participant Audio as AudioService
    participant AM as AudioManager
    participant AudioSrc as AudioSource
    
    Ball->>Bus: Publish(BallContactEvent)
    Bus->>Audio: OnBallContact(event)
    Audio->>AM: Play("BallHit")
    AM->>AudioSrc: Play clip
    AudioSrc-->>Audio: Звук играет
    AudioSrc-->>AM: Звук окончен
```
